General Rules
  • Tournament is exclusively for mobile BGMI players ‚Äî no PC or emulator
  • Unfair tools of any kind are strictly not allowed
  • Each team: 4 main players + 1 optional substitute
  • Only registered players may compete in matches
  • Team names must be clean, appropriate and non-offensive
  • All players must follow admin instructions at all times
Registration Rules
  • Only the In-Game Leader (IGL) fills the registration form
  • One form submission per team ‚Äî duplicates disqualify both
  • Correct BGMI IDs are mandatory for all team members
  • Fake or incorrect IDs = immediate disqualification, no refund
  • No player changes permitted after the match has started
Lobby & Match Rules
  • Room ID and password shared 10 minutes before match start
  • All players must join the lobby before designated time
  • Late entries are automatically disqualified ‚Äî no exceptions
  • Missing players: team must play with remaining members
  • Matches start on schedule ‚Äî zero delays
Game Settings
  • Mode: Squad TPP (Third Person Perspective)
  • Maps: Erangel / Miramar / Rondo (announced per match)
  • All default BGMI match settings apply
Fair Play Rules
  • Hacking or cheating software of any kind ‚Äî zero tolerance
  • Teaming with other squads is strictly prohibited
  • Bug exploitation or glitch abuse not allowed
  • Account sharing forbidden ‚Äî use your registered account only

1. Eligibility

This tournament is open to all mobile BGMI players in India. Players must be 13 years or older. Emulator users and players with active game bans are ineligible.

2. Registration Accuracy

All information submitted during registration must be accurate and truthful. False registration results in immediate disqualification without refund.

3. Payment & Confirmation

Entry fee of ‚Çπ40 per team must be paid before registration is confirmed. Entry fee is strictly non-refundable. Participation confirmed only after payment verification.

4. Tournament Conduct

All participants must maintain respectful and sportsmanlike behaviour. ROG Nation has a zero-tolerance policy for hacking, teaming, account sharing or cheating. Violations result in disqualification and a permanent ban.

5. Admin Authority

All decisions made by ROG Nation admins are final and binding. ROG Nation reserves the right to modify rules, reschedule or cancel the event without prior notice.

6. Liability Disclaimer

ROG Nation is not responsible for technical issues on the participant's end including disconnection, device failure or game crashes.

7. Prize Distribution

Prizes distributed via UPI after result verification. ROG Nation may withhold prizes pending any rule violation investigation. Prize amounts subject to change based on team count.

8. Acceptance of Rules

By registering and participating, all participants acknowledge they have read, understood and agree to abide by all rules set forth by ROG Nation. Participation constitutes full acceptance.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
How do I register for the tournament?
Click "JOIN TOURNAMENT" and fill the official Google Form. Only the IGL submits ‚Äî include all team members' BGMI IDs accurately.
When will the Room ID be shared?
Room ID and password are shared exactly 10 minutes before scheduled match time via our official channels.
Can we change players after registering?
Player changes are only permitted before the match starts. Once a match begins, no substitutions are allowed.
Can we get a refund?
Entry fees are non-refundable. A refund is issued only if ROG Nation cancels the entire tournament.
What happens if we are late to the lobby?
Late entry is not permitted. Teams failing to join on time are automatically disqualified from that match.
What devices are allowed?
Mobile only ‚Äî Android and iOS. Emulators, keyboards, mice or any external peripherals are strictly prohibited.
